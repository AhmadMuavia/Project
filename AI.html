About Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is the branch of computer science that focuses on creating systems capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence. This includes learning, reasoning, problem-solving, perception, and language understanding. Learn more about related fields such as Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Data Science.

AI can be categorized into two main types: Narrow AI and General AI. Narrow AI specializes in a single task, like virtual assistants or recommendation systems, while General AI aims to mimic human-like intelligence across a wide range of activities.

AI has a profound impact across various sectors such as healthcare, finance, education, transportation, and entertainment. It is reshaping how we interact with technology and is poised to drive the next wave of innovation.
